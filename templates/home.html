Computer-assisted scientific inquiry experience

Collaborative Simulation Exploration Activities


In this event we will use CoSci's collaborative simulation to discuss and analyze the physical principles in the simulation. Each inquiry activity will be divided into 4 steps: experience, propose ideas, refine ideas, and verify. Please discuss with your group members and complete them in order.


The topics of this activity include
1.turning。 2.archer。
3.collision。     4.friction。

Activity process:
1. Please use the chat room on the group page to find group members, and click on the table on the left of the group page to join.
2. Afterwards, you can go to your respective group page and use the group’s respective chat room and cooperative simulation to complete the questions in the form step by step!
